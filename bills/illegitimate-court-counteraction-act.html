Illegitimate Court Counteraction Act

Key points about this legislation

  • Sanctions on the ICC: Requires the President to impose property-blocking and visa-related sanctions on any foreign individual involved in the International Criminal Court’s efforts to investigate or prosecute U.S. or allied "protected persons."
  • Funding Restrictions: Immediately rescinds any U.S. funds previously appropriated for the ICC and prohibits future appropriations.
  • Waivers and Termination: Allows the President to waive or lift sanctions under strict conditions, including certification that the ICC has ceased all investigations targeting protected persons.
  • No pork in this bill.
