Nutria Eradication and Control Reauthorization Act

Key points about this legislation

  • Reauthorizes the Nutria Eradication and Control Act of 2003 (nutria: invasive, semi-aquatic rodent) by extending its deadline from 2025 to 2030.
  • Nutria Eradication and Control Act of 2003: Provides financial assistance to States for nutria control and restoration of damaged marshland, wetlands, and agricultural lands, with aid proportional to the impacted area.
  • No pork in this bill.
